Zephyr Quiz League

The Spring Challenge (ZQL S01) will take place across eight weeks from 4 April 2022 until 29 May 2022. The first 6 games will take place in a league format followed by the knockouts: a tier-based semi-final and final.

The Quiz Format...

Each game will be played by four individual participants over Zoom Meetings and will follow a modified MIMIR format - 64 questions will be played over 8 rounds. Questions will feature in both text and visual form.

The league will follow a modified MIMIR format. If youâ€™re new to the MIMIR format, read about it here.

Make sure to attempt the game from a quiet private space with a strong internet connection. Be punctual - join the game at the agreed upon time.

All players are required to be unmuted and keep their videos on during the entire course of the game. Keep both hands visible at all times.

Readers are instructed to warn a player twice if there are any signs of impropriety. Following that, the reader has the liberty to pause the game to reach out to the organisers to make a decision.

There are no tie-breakers during the league stage.

Dealing with no-shows: The players and the reader are requested to wait for any absent player for a maximum of 15 minutes before beginning the game. Make sure to inform the organisers at the 5-minute mark, so that we may attempt to contact the player.
